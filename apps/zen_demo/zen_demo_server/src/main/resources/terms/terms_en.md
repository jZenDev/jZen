# Terms of Service

These are the demonstration terms of service for **jZen**.

`zen_demo` is the reference application: the runnable showcase of jZen and the living
end-to-end test stand. This document is served by `GET /api/v1/demo/ping`'s sibling,
`GET /api/v1/demo/terms`, as localized Markdown, to prove that content is delivered with
language support just like the rest of the surface.

## No mocks

The demo hits the real Quarkus backend and the real Supabase auth stack. Nothing here is a
stub. If something cannot be made real, it does not exist.

## Language

Switch the language between English and Ukrainian to see this document, the ping message,
and the interface change together.
